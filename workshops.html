School Workshops

We are passionate about sharing our love of music—and especially our passion for double-reed instruments—with pupils in schools. Our workshops aim to broaden musical horizons and encourage more young people to discover and take up these lesser-known orchestral instruments.

Sharing the magic of double-reed instruments in the classroom.

Supporting the Music Curriculum

Our sessions support the music curriculum by helping pupils to:

  • Experience live instrumental performance
  • Discover how sound is produced and how instruments work
  • Develop listening skills and musical vocabulary
  • Learn about orchestral instruments and their roles
  • Feel inspired to explore music-making themselves

Flexible Formats

We offer flexible formats to suit your school’s needs:

Assemblies: Lively, engaging presentations introducing the oboe, cor anglais, and bassoon, ideal for whole-school or year-group audiences.

Small-group workshops: More interactive sessions with opportunities for questions, demonstrations, and closer engagement with the instruments.

All workshops are adapted to the age, experience, and learning objectives of the pupils, whether for primary or secondary settings. Our aim is not only to inform, but to inspire curiosity, confidence, and enthusiasm for music—especially for instruments pupils may never have encountered before.

If you would like to discuss workshop options or learning objectives for your school, please get in touch via the Contact Us page or email us at Reediculoustwo@gmail.com.
