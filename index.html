Double the Reeds,
Double the Laughs!

Welcome to the Reed’iculous Two — a duo of oboe and bassoon players bringing music, fun, and creativity to audiences of all ages.

We are a pair of double-reed musicians playing oboe and bassoon. Our mission is to show how expressive (and hilariously fun) these instruments can be — using music, storytelling and comedy to create lively, approachable concerts and memorable workshops for children, families and schools.

Our mission, you ask?

To show the world just how expressive and hilariously fun these instruments can be to listen to, and play. We share a love for creativity, humour, and pushing the boundaries of classical music because we believe it should be enjoyed by everyone! That’s why our performances mix music and comedy to create concerts that are lively, approachable, and full of character.

Who We Are

But performing is only a small part of what we do…

We are also passionate teachers, using our performances to demonstrate our instruments’ abilities and encourage audiences to discover the joy of making music.
